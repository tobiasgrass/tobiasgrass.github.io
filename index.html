About me.

I am a theoretical physicist interested in quantum phenomena and condensed matter physics.

Currently, I work as a Research Fellow at ICFO , in Castelldefels (Barcelona, Spain).

My research focuses on the control of quantum systems - most often by using light-matter interactions. The goal is to simulate complex many-body behavior, to realize exotic effects, and to harness quantum phenomena for real-world applications.

In this context, I am looking at a variety of system: cold atoms and trapped ions, as well as electronic systems including semiconductors and graphene.
